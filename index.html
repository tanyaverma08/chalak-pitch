The Problem

India's Last Mile Is Broken

Metro networks expand. But the final 3km gap between station and home remains chaotic, expensive, and unreliable.

🚧

No Fixed Routes

Auto-rickshaws operate on ad-hoc negotiations. Riders have zero predictability on fare, time, or availability.

💸

Price Gouging

Peak-hour surge pricing up to 3x is routine. Low-income daily commuters pay a disproportionate share of their income.

🌫️

Dead Miles & Pollution

60% of auto trips carry a single passenger. This empty deadhead traffic clogs roads and emits carbon unnecessarily.

📱

Fragmented Supply

15 million autos nationwide have no coordination layer. Each driver operates as an isolated unit with no network benefit.

$8.3B
annual consumer surplus lost to last-mile inefficiency in India's top 10 cities
The Solution

AI-Optimized Shared Auto Network

Chalak turns every willing auto driver into a node in an intelligent transit network — without buying a single new vehicle.

🤖

Real-Time Route Optimization

Our AI engine clusters riders heading the same direction and routes drivers dynamically — maximizing seat utilization to 80%+.

📍

Fixed Fare Transparency

Riders see the fare before booking. No surge, no negotiation. Prices are 35–50% lower than solo auto — and always consistent.

🗺️

Metro-Station Anchoring

Routes radiate from metro and bus terminuses. Chalak completes the first/last mile that public transit cannot — seamlessly.

📲

Driver-First Design

Lightweight driver app works on entry-level Android. Earnings up 28% per driver without extra hours — stronger supply flywheel.

How It Works

Three Steps to a Smarter Ride

1

Rider Opens App

Enters destination. Chalak instantly matches them with riders heading the same way and a nearby driver. Fare shown upfront.

2

AI Clusters & Routes

Our engine solves the vehicle routing problem in under 400ms — assigning pooled pickups and optimizing the shared route in real time.

3

Driver Earns More

Driver fills the seat that would have been empty. Earns 28% more per shift. No new skills needed — just follow the app's turn-by-turn guidance.

4

Network Grows Smarter

Every completed ride feeds the AI with richer demand signal. Routes improve. Availability tightens. Flywheel compounds with scale.
